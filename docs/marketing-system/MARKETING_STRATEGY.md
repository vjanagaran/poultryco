## Content Strategy

### NDP Framework (Need-Desire-Pain)

Content topics are categorized by psychological triggers:

1. **Need:** Essential requirements (e.g., "How to reduce feed costs")
2. **Desire:** Aspirational goals (e.g., "Achieve 95% flock uniformity")
3. **Pain Point:** Current problems (e.g., "High mortality in summer")
4. **Problem:** Systemic issues (e.g., "Inconsistent vaccine supply")
5. **Fear:** Concerns and risks (e.g., "Disease outbreak prevention")
6. **Fantasy:** Ideal outcomes (e.g., "Fully automated farm management")

### Content Workflow (2-Level System)

```
Topic â†’ Pillar â†’ Content â†’ Schedule
```

#### Level 1: Content Pillar
- **Purpose:** Deep research on a core topic
- **Examples:** 
  - PoultryCo Origin Story
  - Complete Guide to Broiler Mortality
  - Feed Optimization Framework
- **Components:**
  - Research question & hypothesis
  - Source documents & references
  - Key insights & findings
  - Target URL for SEO backlinks
  - Focus keywords for SEO

#### Level 2: Content Production
- **Master Content:** Original long-form content
  - Blog posts (1,500-2,500 words)
  - E-books & guides
  - Video content
  - Webinars
  
- **Repurposed Content:** Derived from master
  - Social media posts
  - Infographics
  - Email snippets
  - Quote cards

## SEO Strategy

### Pillar-Level SEO

Each content pillar includes:
- **Target URL:** Specific landing page for backlinks
- **Focus Keywords:** Primary keywords to rank for
- **Content Cluster:** Related content linking back

**Example:**
- **Pillar:** "Complete Guide to Broiler Mortality"
- **Target URL:** `/solutions/broiler-health`
- **Focus Keywords:** "reduce broiler mortality", "broiler health management"
- **Content Cluster:** 4 blogs + 40 social posts all linking back

### Content-Level SEO

Each content piece includes:
- Meta title & description
- SEO-optimized slug
- Focus keywords
- Internal linking strategy
- Image alt text
- Structured data (where applicable)

## Best Practices

### Content Creation
1. Start with audience pain points (NDP framework)
2. Create comprehensive pillars first
3. Repurpose strategically across channels
4. Maintain consistent brand voice
5. Optimize every piece for SEO

### Scheduling
1. Plan 2 weeks ahead minimum
2. Balance content types across channels
3. Consider audience active times
4. Leave room for real-time content
5. Batch similar tasks

### Performance Tracking
1. Review metrics weekly
2. A/B test headlines and formats
3. Double down on what works
4. Learn from underperformers
5. Share insights with team
