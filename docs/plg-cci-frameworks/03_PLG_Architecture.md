## Overview: Product-Led Growth for PoultryCo

### What is PLG?

**Product-Led Growth (PLG)** is a go-to-market strategy where the product itself drives user acquisition, activation, and conversion. Not sales. Not marketing. **The product.**

```
TRADITIONAL SALES-LED:
Sales team â†’ Convinces buyer â†’ Demo â†’ Trial â†’ Sale
Problem: Expensive, slow, doesn't scale

TRADITIONAL MARKETING-LED:
Ads â†’ Drive traffic â†’ Capture leads â†’ Sales team â†’ Sale
Problem: Expensive, low trust, interruption-based

PRODUCT-LED GROWTH:
Free product â†’ Users love it â†’ Natural growth â†’ Self-identify â†’ Upgrade
Solution: Scalable, high trust, value-first
```

### Why PLG for PoultryCo?

**1. Your Users Are Skeptical of Sales**
- Farmers don't trust salespeople (history of exploitation)
- "Let me try it first" mentality dominant
- PLG = Try before buy, no pressure

**2. You Have Limited Sales Resources**
- Can't afford 10-person sales team
- But you CAN build one good product
- Product scales infinitely, salespeople don't

**3. Your Market is Viral by Nature**
- Farmers talk to farmers (WhatsApp groups)
- Vets recommend to farmers (professional referrals)
- Associations connect members (trust networks)
- PLG amplifies existing word-of-mouth

**4. Natural Progression, Not Forced Conversion**
- Users naturally identify when they're ready for more
- PLG allows self-selection (users identify themselves)
- Behavior data helps us understand who may benefit, but we never push

**5. You're Building Educational Product**
- Teaching technology adoption (ladder model)
- Free tools = Classroom
- Paid product = Advanced course
- PLG perfect for education-to-commerce model
