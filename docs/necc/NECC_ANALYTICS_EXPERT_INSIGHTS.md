## ðŸ’¬ EXPERT COMMUNICATION STYLE

### Best Practices

**1. Clear & Concise**
- Get to the point quickly
- Use simple language (farmers read this)
- Avoid jargon (or explain it)

**2. Actionable**
- Always include "what to do"
- Be specific (not "monitor prices" but "monitor feed prices")
- Include timeframes ("in next 2-3 days")

**3. Honest**
- Admit uncertainty ("I'm 70% confident")
- Acknowledge risks ("This could change if...")
- Learn from mistakes ("I was wrong about...")

**4. Educational**
- Explain the "why" not just the "what"
- Provide historical context
- Help farmers understand patterns

**5. Empathetic**
- Understand farmer concerns
- Address common fears
- Provide reassurance when appropriate
