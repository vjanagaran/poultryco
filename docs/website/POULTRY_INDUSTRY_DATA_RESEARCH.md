## üéØ **NETWORK EFFECTS DATA**

### **The Multiplier Concept:**
- **Individual farmer:** Limited market knowledge, isolated
- **5,000 farmers connected:** Crowdsourced prices = accurate data
- **Mathematical:** Network value = n¬≤ (Metcalfe's Law)
- **Practical:** 5,000 farmers > 5,000 WhatsApp groups

**Specific Examples:**
- **More Farmers = Better Prices:**
  - 1 farmer knows local price
  - 5,000 farmers know regional trends
  - Price manipulation becomes impossible

- **More Vets = Faster Help:**
  - Isolated vet: Limited to 15km radius
  - 50 vets networked: Someone always available 24/7
  - Emergency coverage increases

- **More Suppliers = Quality Competition:**
  - Few suppliers: Monopoly pricing
  - 100+ suppliers: Competitive pricing + quality focus

**Source:** Network theory, marketplace dynamics
